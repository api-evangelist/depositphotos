Building effective AI models requires high-quality training datasets that ensure performance, legal safety, and accuracy, and a well-structured smaller dataset can heavily outperform an uncurated dataset ten times its size. The article covers evaluation steps like checking class imbalance, annotation consistency, and documented provenance, and red flags such as web-scraped data marketed under "fair use" and missing IP indemnification. DepositPhotos positions its offering with 330+ million pre-labeled files, multimodal training, and GDPR, CCPA, and EU AI Act compliance.
